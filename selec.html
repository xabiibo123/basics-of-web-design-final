Industrial robotics

The definition of industrial robot and the classification
of robot types determined according to the vISO 8373 Standard are as follows: "An automatically controlled, reprogrammable,
multi-purpose, space-fixed or mobile manipulator with three or more programmable
axes, used in industrial applications."

why you chooseus?

As a leader in industrial automation, OMRON provides solutions that help manufacturers meet customers' specific production needs and address rising labor costs and labor shortages.
