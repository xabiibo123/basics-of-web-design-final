Medi the robot eases fears at
Rahway dentist office

On staff for four years at Olin Dental Group, Medi the robot is programmed to
act as a companion along side children in a dental operatory. Medi is able to speak to
children at their level and decrease their concerns, anxiety and fears
when they come to the dentist. CHERYL MAKIN/STAFF PHOTO
