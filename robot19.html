1. Caution!
1. Exercise extreme caution when opening Ropot’s lid during or after a heating cycle. Always use an oven mitt.
2. Avoid scalding. Never place hands or face over the exhaust vents in the center of the lid when the unit is in operation.
3. Do not touch hot surfaces on lid. Always use the lid handle to open Ropot.
4. Remove inner pot using pot handle. Do not touch any other surface of pot when hot.
5. Exercise extreme caution when moving Ropot containing hot liquids.
6. Do not fill the inner pot above the 3L line.
2. Stop using Ropot if…
1. The power cord or plug suffers from expansion, deformation, discoloration, or other damage.
2. A portion of the power cord or plug becomes otter than usual.
3. Ropot heats abnormally or emits a burnt smell.
4. There are unusual sounds or vibrations when powered on.
